THE BRIEF

Inktober is an annual art challenge during the month of october, where artists draw one ink drawing every day for each day of october and post it on their social media. A prompt list containing 31 prompts for each day of the month would be shared and artists have to draw something related to the prompt for that day. I too participated in the challenge and decided to ink digitally in Adobe Photoshop.

THE DESIGN

I decided to engrave the prompt lettering or idea into my inks by using negative white space. This resulted in designs that required the viewer to look deep to find the interesting bits.
